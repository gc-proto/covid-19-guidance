When to expect your payment

CRHP opens for applications on July 7, 2021, and the first payments should start to be sent the week of July 12.

After the first week of the program opening, you can generally expect to receive your payment within 3 to 8 days, depending on the method of payment:

Processing time without validation

Direct deposit
3 to 8 days, if you are already registered
Cheque
3 to 8 days, plus time for delivery by mail to the address on your payroll account

Processing time with validation

In some cases, we may need to delay your payment if we need to contact you for additional review of your claim.

Register for direct deposit or update your details

Payments over $25 million

If you are expecting a payment of more than $25 million, you need to be registered for both direct deposit and for the Large Value Transfer System (LVTS). To begin the registration process, send LVTS an email at brdlvtsreg@cra-arc.gc.ca.
